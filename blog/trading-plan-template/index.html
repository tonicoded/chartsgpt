Trading Plan Template (Copy/Paste)

Most traders don’t need a new indicator — they need a plan they can execute under pressure. Copy/paste this one-screen template and stop improvising.

Published: February 14, 2026 • Last updated: February 14, 2026 • Reading time: ~7 minutes
Goal: reduce your plan to decisions: if X happens, I do Y, with clear invalidation.
Reality check: no template guarantees profits — but it can remove the mistakes that kill consistency.

How to keep it “clean”

  • Limit levels: 2–4 max.
  • Force two scenarios: no single-bias narratives.
  • Trigger + invalidation: every scenario must have both.
  • Risk first: size is decided before you enter.
