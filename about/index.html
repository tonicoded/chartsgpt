About ChartsGPT

ChartsGPT is built for one job: turn a chart screenshot into a clean, structured plan (levels → scenarios → trigger → invalidation) so you can stop second-guessing.

Last updated: February 14, 2026

What it does

  • Extracts key levels (support/resistance zones)
  • Creates two scenarios (bull + bear) to reduce bias
  • Suggests triggers and invalidation for clarity

What it is not

  • Not financial advice
  • Not a profit guarantee
  • Not a replacement for risk management

About the creator

ChartsGPT is built by an independent creator focused on making chart analysis more structured and less emotional. The goal is simple: help you turn screenshots into repeatable plans with clear triggers, invalidation, and risk.
